## Overview
Start documenting your project in just a few minutes.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Your First Page">
    ````markdown
    Click "Add Page" in your project space, name your doc, and write your content using markdown.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    ````markdown
    Hit the "Publish" button. Your page goes live instantly for your team.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
